Data coverage
Pendo events and account counts are sourced from product area activity in the AlayaCare web app (Marketplace, Data Intelligence, RCM, Admission & Care Delivery, Workforce Management) — not individual feature-level tags. Exception: Data Exploration 2 uses page-level views (Pendo page: "Data Exploration 2.0 | AlayaCare Cloud") which tracks direct URL visits. MOB tickets (care worker app) live in separate Pendo apps (Caregiver iOS/Android) and were not queried. CC Activated = % of eligible tenants with the feature on in Control Center as of Jul 6, 2026.
Data Exploration 2 — quality issues in a heavily-used feature
58,708 Pendo page views across 834 accounts and 6,596 unique visitors — DE 2.0 is one of the most-accessed features in this report. Yet 167 Zendesk tickets report slowness, incorrect calculations, and unavailability. This is not a dormant feature: customers are using it and hitting quality issues. Only 21% of eligible tenants have it activated (236/1,107) — fixing quality should unlock significant further adoption.
Visit Optimizer — C4 quality spike (May 13 – Jun 2)
21 of 46 total Zendesk tickets arrived during the C4 window — wrong discipline assignments, inaccurate travel time and data. Volume dropped to 8 in C5 and 1 in C6, suggesting a fix was applied. Confirm with the LAB team whether a hotfix was shipped.
Field User Portal — silent regression
Zero Zendesk tickets, but Pendo shows 82K "return to legacy" clicks vs. 9K "try enhanced" — a 9:1 reverse ratio. 73% of eligible tenants have it activated; most users are silently opting back out. Worth a targeted survey or in-app guide to understand the friction.

Activation × Usage — what the Control Center tells us

High activation + low Pendo = discoverability gap. Low activation = onboarding opportunity. High ZD + no Pendo = quality blocker.

High activation · low Pendo — discoverability gap
AI Voice-to-Form (Dictate) — 100% activated (all 16 eligible tenants on), but only 42 Pendo accounts and 1,634 events in 92 days. Every eligible customer unlocked it; almost none of their users are finding it. Needs in-app discovery or onboarding guidance.
AI Smart Summaries — 95% activated, Moderate Pendo signal. High reach but usage is purposeful rather than frequent — may be working as designed.
Field User Portal — 73% activated, yet 9:1 reverse-click ratio. Three-quarters of eligible tenants turned it on; most users are opting back to legacy.
Low activation · urgent action needed
Marketplace — only 11% of eligible tenants activated (11/99). The Strong Pendo signal is coming from a very small base. 89 eligible tenants haven't switched it on — significant untapped growth.
Data Exploration 2 — 21% activated (236/1,107) yet 58K Pendo page views across 834 accounts. The activated cohort is using it heavily, but 79% of eligible tenants haven't turned it on. Fix quality first (167 ZD tickets), then push activation.
Notable — 49% activated (211/430). Half of eligible customers haven't enabled it. Strong Pendo signal for those who have — good candidate for an activation campaign.
